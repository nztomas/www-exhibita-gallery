About

Exhibit A Gallery is located in the historic Mangonui Courthouse on the picturesque Waterfront Drive. Built in 1892, it served as the local courthouse until 1948, becoming a historic reserve in 1980. After much conservation work, today the kauri building has a new lease of life showcasing the work of some of the region's finest arts and crafts people.
